Iphone 17 Pro Max

Key Features

Display & Design

  • 6.9-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display with HDR support and high peak brightness (up to 3000 nits outdoors) for vibrant visuals.

  • ProMotion adaptive refresh rate up to 120 Hz for smoother scrolling and responsiveness.

  • Dynamic Island interactive display area and Always-On display for glanceable info.

  • Premium aluminium unibody with Ceramic Shield 2 front and back.

Camera System

  • Triple 48 MP “Fusion” rear cameras — wide, ultra-wide and telephoto — offering sharp, detailed photos.

  • Large telephoto lens with up to 8× optical-quality zoom and up to 40× digital zoom.

  • Advanced computational photography with Photonic Engine, Smart HDR, and Deep Fusion.

  • 18 MP Centre Stage front camera that expands the field of view and adds ultra-stabilised video capture.

Performance & Software

  • Powered by Apple’s A19 Pro chip with a 6-core CPU, 6-core GPU, and 16-core Neural Engine — very fast performance for apps, games, and AI tasks.

  • Integrated Apple Intelligence features throughout iOS for smarter on-device experiences.

Battery & Charging

  • One of the best battery lives ever in an iPhone, with long video playback times.

  • Fast-charging support (up to 50% in ~20 minutes with a compatible adapter).

Connectivity & Extras

  • 5G, Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 6, Ultra Wideband, and Express Cards with Power Reserve support.

  • IP68 water and dust resistance.

  • Face ID enabled through TrueDepth sensor.

Other Notable Features

  • Large storage options up to 2 TB.

  • Advanced video features including 4K HDR, ProRes and Dual Capture (front + rear simultaneously).

  • Emergency SOS and Satellite features.
