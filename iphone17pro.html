Iphone 17 Pro

Key Features

Design & Display

  • Premium aluminium unibody build with Ceramic Shield 2 on both front and back for industry-leading durability.

  • 6.3-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display with ProMotion adaptive refresh up to 120 Hz, Always-On display, and up to 3,000 nits peak brightness — excellent outdoors.

  • Dynamic Island for system alerts and interactions.

Performance

  • Powered by Apple’s A19 Pro chip — the most powerful iPhone processor yet, with major gains in sustained performance for gaming, video editing, and AI tasks.

  • Advanced Apple N1 networking chip supporting Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 6, and faster connectivity.

Camera System

  • Triple 48 MP Fusion rear cameras — Main, Ultra Wide, and a next-generation Telephoto lens with up to 8× optical-quality zoom — the longest optical quality zoom ever on an iPhone.

  • New 18 MP Centre Stage front camera with intelligent framing and ultra-stable video.

  • Pro-level photography and filmmaking features like ProRes RAW, Apple Log 2, and genlock support.

Battery & Charging

  • Bigger battery with improved power efficiency — significantly better battery life than prior generations and fast charging support (50 % in ~20 min with compatible adapter).

Storage Options

  • Available in 256 GB, 512 GB, and 1 TB capacities.
