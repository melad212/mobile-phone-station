Iphone 17

Key Features

Display & Design

  • 6.3-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display with ProMotion adaptive refresh up to 120 Hz for smoother scrolling and responsiveness.

  • Always-On display and Dynamic Island integration.

  • Ceramic Shield 2 front glass with enhanced scratch resistance and reduced glare.

  • Sleek, durable aluminium frame and colour-infused glass back in multiple finishes.

Performance

  • Powered by the A19 chip — Apple’s latest processor with 6-core CPU, 5-core GPU, and a 16-core Neural Engine for fast, efficient performance, gaming, and AI-enabled tasks.

  • All-day battery life with fast charging (up to ~50% in about 20 min with a compatible adapter).

Camera System

  • 48 MP Dual Fusion camera system (main and ultra-wide) for high-resolution photos and video.

  • Centre Stage front camera improves selfies and video calls with intelligent framing and higher detail.

  • Advanced photo and video features including HDR video, cinematic modes, and improved low-light capture.

Smart Features & Connectivity

  • Apple Intelligence integrated across the system, with AI-driven tools for tasks like translation, photo cleanup, and more.

  • Supports 5G, Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 6, and enhanced wireless performance (via Apple’s new N1 networking chip).

Storage & Options

  • Storage options typically start at 256 GB and go up to 512 GB.
